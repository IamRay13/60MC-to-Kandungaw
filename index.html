Adventure Guide: Kandungaw Peak, Osmeña Peak, and Obong Spring

Day 1: May 10 (Saturday)

  • 5:00 AM: Meet-Up at One Montage
  • 5:30 AM to 7:30 AM: Travel to Maloray Drop-Off
  • 7:30 AM: Arrival at Maloray Drop-Off
  • 8:00 AM to 9:30 AM: Start Hike to Kandungaw Peak
  • 9:30 AM to Sunset: Time at Kandungaw Peak

Day 2: May 11 (Sunday)

  • 6:00 AM: Wake-Up Call & Breakfast
  • 10:00 AM: Back Trail to Maloray
  • 11:30 AM to 12:30 PM: Travel from Maloray to Osmeña Peak
  • 12:30 PM to 1:30 PM: Hike & Explore Osmeña Peak
  • 1:30 PM to 2:00 PM: Travel from Osmeña Peak to Obong Spring
  • 2:00 PM to 5:00 PM: Relax at Obong Spring
  • 5:30 PM Onward: Wrap-Up & Return

Essentials to Bring

  • Comfortable hiking clothes, jacket, and extra change of clothes.
  • Lightweight tent, sleeping bag, and portable stove.
  • At least 2 liters of water per person and energy snacks.
  • Flashlight, first aid kit, sunscreen, and insect repellent.
  • Trash bags, camera, and cash for fees.
